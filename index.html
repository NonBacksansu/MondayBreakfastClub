About Us

Our Story

Monday Breakfast Club was born from a simple idea: Mondays shouldn't be boring. We created a space where the breakfast club culture thrives—a place where young professionals, remote workers, and friends can gather over exceptional food and coffee.

Our Mission

To deliver quality breakfast using local ingredients, creating a welcoming, Instagram-worthy space with fast, friendly service.
